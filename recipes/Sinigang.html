Sinigang

Description

Sinigang--The famous Filipino sour soup. Some people hate and other love it, but when the affinity is apparent, it's strong.

Ingredients:

    1 lbs of pork, cubed 4 cups of water 1 packet of sinigang powder 1/4 lb of chinese spinach or bok choy 2 tomatoes 6 small taro root fish sauce

Steps:

    In a large pot, Add water to pot and allow to boil. Once water is boiling, add meat. Allow to cook until no pink is showing. Next, add taro root and let boil for 15 minutes Stir in one packet of sinigang powder mix. Optionally, add 2 thai peppers for spice Add spinach or bok choy a few minutes prior to serving to soften vegetable To serve, first add one taro and enough soup to a bowl of rice Smash taro and mix with rice and soup until a creamy amalgamation is created Add desired amount of fish sauce and continue to mix Lastly, top with meat and veggies and enjoy
