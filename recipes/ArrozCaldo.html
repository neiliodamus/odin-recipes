Arroz Caldo

Description

Arroz Caldo is one of my favorite childhood recipes. It is THE ideal meal for a cold or rainy day, as it will warm you up and change the mood almost insantly

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 1/2 lb of chicken legs
  • 5 cups of water
  • 3 tbsp of ginger
  • 4 tbsp of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • 1 dozen eggs
  • calamansi (or lemon)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, add olive oil, chopped ginger and 2 tbsp of chopped garlic
  2. Once ginger and garlic have browned, add in chicken legs. Cook thoroughly and wait till chicken is brown
  3. Add water to pot and allow to boil
  4. Once water is boiling, add rice, black pepper, and fish sauce, and stir
  5. Allow to cook for 15 minutes or until rice is soft
  6. In another pan, add olive oil. Once hot, add shopped garlic. Cook till crisped but not burnt
  7. Scoop desired amount into bowl, and add chopped green onions, fried garlic, and sliced eggs as toppings
  8. Squeeze 1/4 of calamansi and add fish sauce
