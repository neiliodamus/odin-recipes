Sinigang

Description

Chicken Adobo is a classic filipino dish liked by all. Simple too make but complex in flavors and experience.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1.5 lbs of chicken legs or thighs
  • 3 tbsp of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of onions sliced
  • 1/2 cup of white vinegar
  • 2 cups of soy sauce
  • 4 bay leaf
  • black pepper

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, add olive oil, garlic, and onions. Allow to brown.
  2. Once brown, add chicken evenly across bottom of pot. Cook each drumstick evenly.
  3. Once brown, add soy sauce, white vinegar, bay leaf, and black pepper. Allow to simmer for 15 minutes on low-medium heat
  4. To serve, pour over a bed of rice and enjoy
